Chill... Rules are simple

This game is by default set for 3 points, the one who gets 3points first will win the game.If you wish to play for more points you can change it on home page .

On the count of 4 (Rock,Paper,Scissor and Shoot!) you need to make a decision on choosing either rock or paper or scissor.

1. player who chooses rock will beat another player who chooses scissors("rock crushes scissors").

2. player who chooses scissor will beat another player who chooses paper("scissors cut paper").

3. player who chooses paper will beat another player who chooses rock("paper covers rock").

4. both players choose the same sign then it is a tie.

5. A player who doesn't choose an option and their score is greater than zero, '1' point will be deducted from their score.

Summary: In simple words,A player who decides to play rock will beat another player who chooses scissors ("rock crushes scissors"), but will lose to one who has played paper ("paper covers rock"); a play of paper will lose to a play of scissors ("scissors cuts paper"). If both players choose the same shape, the game is tied, but is usually replayed until there is a winner.
